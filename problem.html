THE PROBLEM & THE SOLUTION

THE PROBLEM

About 85% of clothing and 13 million tons of textiles is thrown away each year in America. There are substantial amounts of clothing being thrown away for various reasons and it should be going to those who do not have clothes to wear because they cannot afford it.

THE SOLUTION

Charity4All allows the user to giveaway their unwanted clothing to those who are in need. C4L gives the user access to see in which category their clothes will be most beneficial to.
